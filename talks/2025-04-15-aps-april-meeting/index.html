Taiji Mission Status and Science Prospects

Date: April 15, 2025
Speaker: Wu Yueliang (Institute of Theoretical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences)
Event: APS April Meeting 2025
Location: Sacramento, California, USA
Type: Conference
Keywords: mission design, science objectives, pathfinder results
View Slides

Abstract

An overview of the current status of the Taiji space gravitational wave detection mission, including recent results from the Taiji-2 pathfinder and plans for the full constellation launch. The talk will cover key science objectives, technical challenges, and expected scientific returns.

This plenary talk at the APS April Meeting provided the international community with the latest updates on Taiji mission development and highlighted the complementary science capabilities with LISA.
