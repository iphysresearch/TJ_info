Taiji Data Challenge Workshop

Date: December 10, 2024
Speaker: Gong Xuefei (Institute of Mechanics, Chinese Academy of Sciences)
Event: Taiji Data Challenge Workshop 2024
Location: Hangzhou, China
Type: Workshop
Keywords: data challenge, data analysis, community engagement
View Slides

Abstract

Introduction to the Taiji Data Challenge, a community effort to develop and test data analysis algorithms for the Taiji mission. The workshop covers challenge datasets, evaluation metrics, and opportunities for international collaboration.

The Taiji Data Challenge provides realistic simulated datasets to the community, fostering algorithm development and international collaboration in preparation for Taiji science operations.
