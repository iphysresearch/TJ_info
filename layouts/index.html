Taiji Publications & Talks

A comprehensive collection of publications and presentations related to the Taiji space gravitational wave detection project.

Taiji is a Chinese space-based gravitational wave detection mission designed to observe gravitational waves in the millihertz frequency band. The mission aims to detect signals from massive black hole binaries, extreme mass ratio inspirals, and other astrophysical sources.
